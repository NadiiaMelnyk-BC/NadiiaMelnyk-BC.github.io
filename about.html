About Me

Business Central developer, ERP migration specialist, and continuous learner.

Background

I am a Microsoft Dynamics NAV / 365 Business Central developer with deep expertise in ERP migration and customization. My work focuses on helping organizations transition from legacy NAV systems to modern Business Central environments — ensuring data integrity, process continuity, and minimal disruption.

I build custom AL extensions, design financial reporting solutions, implement API integrations, and manage the complex details of permissions, licensing, and data migration. Whether it is upgrading from C/AL to AL, refactoring legacy customizations, or building entirely new functionality, I approach every project with attention to both technical quality and business impact.

I believe in sharing knowledge openly. This site serves as both my professional portfolio and a resource for other BC/NAV developers navigating similar challenges.
